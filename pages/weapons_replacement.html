NAVAL MINES
🇮🇷
EM-52 / Sea Mines Arsenal
Iran possesses approximately 6,000 sea mines — the world's third-largest naval mine inventory. Mix of contact mines, influence mines (acoustic, magnetic, pressure), and smart mines. The EM-52 "rocket-propelled" mine is particularly dangerous. Strategically positioned, these can close the Strait of Hormuz to tanker traffic, triggering a global oil shock. Primarily deployed by IRGC Navy submarines and fast boats.
