Assessment Summary

The Korean Peninsula remains a high-risk armistice theater where limited conventional actions can rapidly become nuclear signaling events. The most important watch items are ballistic missile tests, tactical nuclear deployment signals, DMZ artillery posture, cyber and space activity, USFK/ROK exercise cycles, and whether a Taiwan contingency draws US attention or assets away from Korea.

Priority Indicators

SRBM / MRBM LaunchesLauncher dispersal plus political demands is more important than a routine single test.
600mm MLRSWatch for forward artillery posture near Seoul, ammunition movement, and command delegation signals.
Punggye-ri / YongbyonNuclear-site reporting matters most when paired with crisis rhetoric, cyber disruption, or missile activity.
SOF / Drone IncidentsSmall incursions can become escalation probes when synchronized with artillery, cyber, or information operations.
USFK / Freedom ShieldExercise responses reveal alliance readiness, public messaging discipline, and proportional-response options.
China Border SupportBorder logistics, diplomatic cover, and air-defense posture can turn a Korea crisis into a regional crisis.
