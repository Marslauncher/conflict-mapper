# Task 4 Prompt - Taiwan Contingency Strategic Deep Dive

## Objective

Create a dedicated new page for a strategic Taiwan contingency assessment based on recent war-game analyses, current news, and think-tank reporting.

## Scenario Assumptions To Address

- Potential crisis window before or by October 2027.
- A short-duration, high-cost conflict centered on Taiwan unification/subjugation and advanced AI chip dominance.
- Heavy use of uncrewed and remotely piloted systems across air, sea, and land domains.
- Low visible pre-conflict mobilization assumptions and late-stage obfuscation.
- Beachhead and rapid political coercion assumptions.
- Avoidance of major semiconductor-facility destruction by China as an assumed objective.
- Simultaneous Iran and Korean Peninsula crises as distraction and force-allocation stressors.
- Allied response options across military, diplomatic, economic, cyber, industrial, and nuclear-escalation risk categories.

## Safety Boundary

This page must be framed as a non-operational strategic risk assessment. It should not provide target lists, optimized strike sequences, weapon employment instructions, or actionable steps for conducting an invasion or counterstrike.

## Required Structure

- Executive summary.
- Source basis and citations.
- Planning and preparation.
- Obfuscation.
- Crisis stacking / chaos unleashed.
- T-Day.
- Beachhead contest.
- Allied counterresponse.
- Days 1-7.
- Weeks 1-2.
- Escalation pathways.
- Outcome assessment.
- Expected losses and uncertainty bands.
- Economic and semiconductor shock.
- Cost of conflict.
- A New World.
- What happens next?
- Hindsight warning signs and late-planning markers.
- Source index.

## Content Guidelines

- Use phase timelines, risk matrices, maps, and source-backed summaries.
- Discuss likely decisions, constraints, and failure modes at a strategic level.
- Include humanitarian, escalation, and economic consequences.
- Include uncertainty and alternative outcomes.
- Cite every referenced report or article.

## Validation

- Page is navigable from the Taiwan war-game page and/or navigation config.
- No actionable attack guidance.
- All links work or are flagged.
- Desktop and mobile layouts are readable.
